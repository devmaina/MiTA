About MiTA

MiTA - Man in the Arena is a groundbreaking 3-day event designed to forge a unique bond between the army and the citizen. It's an opportunity for individuals to step up, test their mettle alongside military personnel, and experience the rigor and discipline of high-intensity physical challenges.

Beyond the competition, MiTA celebrates strength, resilience, and community. Participants and spectators will enjoy vibrant entertainment, including music and shows, culminating in an awards ceremony where top performers are recognized with prestigious trophies. MiTA is more than just an event; it's a testament to the spirit of endurance and national unity.
